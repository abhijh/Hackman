About Us

The first of its kind from the Department of Information Science and Engineering, DSCE, Hackman is a 24 hour Open-Hack from 15th to the 16th of October. We promise you loads of fun, entertainment, unlimited internet and food. Apart from the usual 'good for your resume' and 'great platform to present your ideas', there are a lot of exciting prizes up for grabs and your crazy ideas are certain to leave a lasting impression. And like that wasn't enough, when word of your work reaches out to the community gathered at Hackman (be it your thought process or your creative ability), you may have just landed yourself an exciting internship that would save you from that stressful placement process. (T&C apply as always, right?) This could be the best tech experience in your engineering life and we promise to give you a reason to come back next year! We will be in DSI on the 15th. Where will you be?
